My projects_

HealthBit

Over decades, medical facilities have evolved elegantly. Still most of us are the witness of the fact that whenever we see a doctor, we need to put forward our medical file in front of him/her. Our file contains our previous prescriptions, medical reports,etc. It is a tedious task to keep record of all these. We provided a digital solution to this problem so that, next time when you visit your doctor, you don’t need to carry your medical file. We have developed Web portal to allow the doctor to view the past records of any patient with the patient’s consent.
